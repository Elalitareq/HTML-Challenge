    Why should you eat potatoes?!

    Potatoes contain vitamins, minerals, and fiber.

    - They are rich in vitamin C, which is also an anti-oxidents;
    - In addition to that, potatoes contain potassium, which is good for the heart, muscles and neurons;
    - Even the potatoes' skin contain fibers that are good for a person's health;
    - Different types of potatoes have also additional nutrients, ie: sweet potatoes cotain vitamin A.

    How to eat potatoes.

    Potatoes can be eaten in multiple ways:

    - It can be coocked with other ingridients like meat, soups or other vegetables;
    - It can be cut into pieces and fried (French Fries);
    - It can also be cut and baked;
    - It can also be boiled and made into mashed potatoes;
    - Also you can make Potato Widges;
    - In addition, it can be used to create buns for burgers;
